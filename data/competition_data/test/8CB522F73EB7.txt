Drivers should not be able to use their cell phone while driving because, it can provide a safety issue and cause bad behaviors from the other drivers.

Driving while texting or calling someone can result in a terrible car accident because the phone distracts the driver from the road causing them to not pay attention to their surroundings. This is the reason we now have hand free hand free technology to decrease the amount of distracted drivers.

When a driver is on their phone at a red light this can cause them to not pay attention to the stop light which means the other drivers are most likely to get mad and result into nasty behavior such as blowing the horn or simply putting their selves in jeopardy by going around the other person.

The use of cell phones or any other electronic device should not be able to be used while driving.
